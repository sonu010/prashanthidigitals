## 💡 Tips for Taking/Preparing Photos

1. **Landscape orientation** for most images (horizontal)
2. **High resolution** — take at highest quality, we'll resize for web
3. **Good lighting** — well-lit photos look best on website
4. **Consistent style** — try to maintain similar color tones across photos
5. **Compress before upload** — use WebP or JPEG at 80% quality
6. **No text on photos** — the website adds text overlays
7. **LED wall photos** — try to capture the full screen with content visible
8. **Owner photo** — professional shot, either in studio or working at an event
